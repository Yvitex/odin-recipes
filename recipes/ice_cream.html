Ice Cream

Description

Cold and flavorful ice cream that melts in the mouth with customizable colors

Ingredient:

  • 1 can of (DW 507g) fruit cocktail, drained
  • 1 pc of 125ml NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream, chilled
  • ⅓ cup of Nestle® Carnation® Condensada

Steps:

  1. Purée fruit cocktail with Nestle® Carnation® Condensada until coarsely chopped.
  2. Whip NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream until stiff peaks.
  3. Fold whipped cream into fruit cocktail mixture and transfer on a freezer-safe container. Freeze overnight. Scoop and serve immediately.
